Cybercity is a rapidly evolving urban concept in the current digital era. It is a manifestation of the digital transformation that has changed the way we live, work, and interact. In Cybercity, information technology and communication play a central role in managing urban infrastructure, providing public services, and enhancing the quality of life for its residents.In Cybercity, ultra-fast internet connectivity and easy access to various smart devices have become the norm. Smart and innovative Smart City policies have been implemented to improve energy efficiency, transportation, and waste management. This leads to a reduction in carbon footprint and the creation of greener and more sustainable cities.One crucial aspect of Cybercity is the Internet of Things (IoT) concept, where various devices and sensors are connected in a network that functions to collect and share real-time data. This enables better monitoring and control in various sectors such as healthcare, security, and the environment.In Cybercity, residents can access government services online, keep track of city developments through mobile applications, and participate in more democratic decision-making.
